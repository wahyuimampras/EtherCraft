About Ether Craft

Crafting Premium Keyboard Experiences Since Day One

Our Story

Founded in Jakarta, Indonesia, Ether Craft emerged from a simple belief: every keystroke should be an experience. We saw a gap in the market between mass-produced keyboards and truly premium, customizable options that cater to enthusiasts who demand more.

What started as a passion project among keyboard enthusiasts has grown into Jakarta's premier destination for mechanical keyboard aficionados. We're not just selling products; we're building a community of people who appreciate the art and science of typing.

Our team combines technical expertise with an eye for aesthetics, carefully curating every product in our catalog. We believe that your keyboard is more than just a tool—it's an extension of your personality, your workspace, and your creative process.

Our Mission & Values

Premium Quality

We hand-select every product, ensuring it meets the highest standards of craftsmanship and performance. No compromises, no mass-market mediocrity—only the finest mechanical keyboards, keycaps, and switches that deliver exceptional typing experiences.

Exclusive Experience

Our collection features limited edition designs and artisan creations you won't find elsewhere. We partner with talented makers worldwide to bring unique, collectible pieces to the Indonesian keyboard community, ensuring your setup is truly one-of-a-kind.

Community-Centric

Built by enthusiasts, for enthusiasts. We understand the keyboard community's needs because we're part of it. From beginner guidance to expert customization, we're here to support your journey into the world of mechanical keyboards.

What We Offer

A Comprehensive Range of Premium Products and Services

Premium Keycaps

Transform your keyboard with artisan-designed, collectible keycaps. From hand-crafted resin art pieces to complete PBT sets, each keycap is selected for its quality, aesthetics, and durability.

  • Artisan resin keycaps
  • Complete keycap sets
  • PBT double-shot construction
  • Limited edition designs

Custom Mechanical Keyboards

Premium aluminum cases, hot-swappable PCBs, and customizable layouts. Our keyboards are built for enthusiasts who want complete control over their typing experience.

  • Various layouts (60%, 65%, 75%, TKL)
  • Hot-swap PCB support
  • Premium aluminum cases
  • Programmable firmware

High-Performance Switches

Choose from an extensive selection of mechanical switches to match your typing preference. Linear for speed, tactile for feedback, or clicky for that satisfying sound.

  • Linear switches (smooth, quiet)
  • Tactile switches (feedback bump)
  • Clicky switches (audible click)
  • Premium and enthusiast options

Custom Build Service

Let our experts build your dream keyboard. From component selection to professional assembly and tuning, we handle every detail with precision and care.

  • Personalized consultation
  • Professional assembly
  • Switch lubing and tuning
  • Quality assurance testing
